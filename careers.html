Careers at CeyloanBot

Join our team and be part of an innovative fintech company empowering Sri Lanka through crypto finance. We are looking for passionate individuals to help us grow and make a difference.

Open Positions

  • Software Developer – Build and maintain our platform.
  • Marketing Specialist – Promote our brand and grow our community.

How to Apply

Send your CV and cover letter to ceyloanbot@proton.me with the subject line “Job Application - [Position Name]”.

Include relevant experience, skills, and why you want to join CeyloanBot.
